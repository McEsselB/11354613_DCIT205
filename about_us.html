Head of Department's welcome-message

Welcome to the vibrant Department of Computer Sciences at the University of Ghana. Our department is the hub for diverse computer and computational sciences programs within UG. Aligned with the evolving needs of Ghana and the broader West African Sub-region, we uphold our core values of excellence, quality, and interconnectedness. Our primary focus is to impart relevant education to our students, extend consultancy and training to our stakeholders, and pioneer groundbreaking research in various computing domains. Our endeavors are powered by cutting-edge facilities and a faculty encompassing diverse expertise in Information Technology and Computing. I take immense pride in inviting you to explore this platform. Whether as a student, researcher, or industry professional, I eagerly anticipate welcoming you to our vibrant community.

Our organization is committed to leveraging computer science knowledge and skills to positively impact local and global communities through outreach programs, solutions, and initiatives.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana is one of Africa's most preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region, and the world is profound, whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000 students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us. For more about our impact, visit our news site, UG News.
